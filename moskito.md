# Moskito

An alcohol free version of the Mochito cocktail.

*vegan*

**1 Portion**

---

- *1/2* lime
- *5* mint leaves
- *1* mint sprig
- *2 TS* cane sugar
- *20 g* crushed ice
- *10 cl* ginger ale

---

Add lime, cane sugar and mint into a longdrink glas and crush.
Add crushed ice and fill with ginger ale.
